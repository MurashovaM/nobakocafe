Healthy-Raw-Vegan-Hand made desserts

Our desserts will definitely appeal to you if you appreciate dishes made with only natural ingredients.

The uniqueness of No-bake Café lies in full involvement of our pastry-chefs in the preparation. This allows to create a dessert that conveys the chef's love for ingredients and our love for you.

We combine local and tropical fruit, nuts, dried fruit, seeds, vegetable milk, spices, teas and other superfood in our preparation. You won't find microwave or any other ovens, pans in our kitchen, because the magic of cooking happens without heat and chemical treatment. Even such processes as chocolate tempering are limited to 42 °C in our kitchen.

Our desserts are made without use of animal fats, refined sugar or wheat flour! This allows our sweets to be filling, tasty, light and to give you energy for your new achievements. Slow carbohydrates are magically transformed into pure energy, which is an indisputable value of any high-quality and complete dessert.

We love our guests and care for them!
